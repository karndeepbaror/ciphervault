# CipherVault - Frequently Asked Questions

**Q:Does CipherVault send data to any server?**.  
*A:* No. All processing happens locally in your browser.

**Q: Is my data stored online?**.    
*A:* No, there is zero transmission or storage on servers.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**.     
*A:* Yes, once loaded, it works without internet.

**Q: What encryption standard is used?**.    
*A:* AES-256, a military-grade standard.

**Q: Is this suitable for enterprise use?**.   
*A:* Yes, designed for personal and professional security.
